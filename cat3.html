Category 3 Hurricane

Winds 111-130 mph (96-113 kt)

Some structural damage to small residences and utility buildings.

Large trees blown down. Mobile homes and poorly built signs destroyed.

Flooding near the coast destroys smaller structures with larger structures

damaged by floating debris. Terrain may be flooded well inland.

Examples: Opal 1995 Alicia 1983
